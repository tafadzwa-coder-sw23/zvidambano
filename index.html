Our Products

Maize (Corn)

High-quality maize (corn) sourced directly from local farmers and large-scale producers. Our maize is ideal for milling, animal feed production, and various food industries, ensuring high nutritional value and consistent grain size. Essential for food security and industrial use in Zimbabwe.

Wheat

Premium milling wheat, carefully selected for its high protein content and excellent baking characteristics. Ideal for flour production for bakeries, pasta manufacturers, and various food processing companies, ensuring consistent quality.

Soybeans

Versatile soybeans supplied with stringent quality control. Perfect for oil extraction, protein-rich animal feed, and a wide range of food applications, including tofu and soy milk production. Our soybeans meet diverse market requirements.

Sorghum

Nutrient-rich sorghum, a drought-resilient crop and a staple in many traditional diets. Available for various market demands, including brewing, animal feed, and direct human consumption due to its gluten-free properties.

Pearl Millet

Highly nutritious and drought-resistant pearl millet, a key grain for food and feed applications, especially in arid regions. Sourced responsibly to ensure consistent supply and exceptional quality for our clients.

Groundnuts (Peanuts)

High-quality groundnuts for snacking, oil production, and confectionery. Our groundnuts meet diverse market requirements, known for their rich flavor and high oil content, delivered with full traceability.
